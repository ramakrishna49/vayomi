Origin

Where our story begins

VAYOME began as a small idea in a studio filled with fabrics, pins and sketches — to create boutique Indian wear that feels light to wear, rich to look at, and lasting in memory.

Every collection still starts at that same place — drapes pinned on a mannequin, colors held up to daylight and outfits imagined on real celebrations.

Philosophy

Our philosophy

We design with three anchors in mind — comfort, elegance and emotion. Every VAYOME outfit must do all three.

Comfort-first luxury — soft, lightweight drapes that move with you through long functions and camera flashes.
Quiet statement — understated shimmer, clean silhouettes and details that feel rich without ever being loud.
Made with intention — each piece is designed with a mood, an occasion and a woman in mind, never just a SKU.
Craft

Fabric, fall & finish

We work with handpicked silks, organzas, georgettes and blended weaves, tested for drape and comfort.

Each border, lace, tassel and stitch line is considered — how it falls, how it photographs, and how it feels after hours of wear.
